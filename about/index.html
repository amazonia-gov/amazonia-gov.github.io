The Flag:

The national flag of Amazonia consists four vertical stripes of gold and blue and a white square in the center with a blue pattern from the Emblem. Gold symbolizes prosperity and honor, blue symbolizes liberty and future, white symbolizes egality and peace.


The Emblem:

The national emblem of amazonia has a white aspis shield with blue pattern, surrounded by olive branches.
