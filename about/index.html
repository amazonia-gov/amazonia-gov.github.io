About Amazonia

The Republic of Amazonia is an independent sovereign state located in Amazonis Planitia on Mars. The nation was first established in December 2020 by Vân Tinh Nghiên Tuyết. Amazonia is bordered by Martian Olympus to the east, and Southern Mars in the south.

Official Name:

Most serene Republic of Amazonia (English)
阿玛宗最尊贵共和国 (Chinese)
Cộng hòa tôn quý nhất Amazonia (Vietnamese)

Area:

4,386,898 km2

Capital city:

Picaflofia

Establishment history:

Independence from Federated States of Martian Olympus: 28 December 2020
The Republic of Amazonia Declaration of Independence signed: 9 August 2021

The Flag:

The national flag of Amazonia consists of three horizontal stripes, with the middle stripe in gold being twice the height of the top and bottom naval blue stripes, charged with a naval blue symbol of Artemis in the center. The gold stripe symbolizes prosperity and honor, and the blue stripes symbolizes peace and the future.

The Emblem:

The national emblem of amazonia has a white aspis shield with blue pattern, surrounded by olive branches.
