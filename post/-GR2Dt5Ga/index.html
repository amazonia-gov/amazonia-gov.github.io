3 commemorative NFTs issued for Amazonia's first anniversary

· 29-12-2021 · # Economy # Tech # News

Happy 1st independence day!
The Most Serene Republic of Amazonia was formed on 28 December 2020, It's been a whole year till now. The President Her Serene Highness Vân Tinh Nghiên Tuyết issued 3 commemorative NFTs on Rinkeby test network, to mark this wonderful event.
The President said that this is the first time that MSRA has released NFTs. Although it is not large in scale, it is a good start. In the future, the MSRA government will make more NFTs for everyone to collect.
